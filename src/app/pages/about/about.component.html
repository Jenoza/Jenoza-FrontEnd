Our Story

Founded in 2020 and proudly based in the UAE, Jenoza emerged with a single mission: to be the ultimate partner for brands ready to stand out. What began as a creative studio has grown into a multi-disciplinary branding agency delivering end-to-end digital and media solutions. Today, we empower startups, scale-ups, and enterprises with the tools to tell their story, connect with their audience, and lead their industry.
